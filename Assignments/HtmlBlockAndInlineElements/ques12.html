Inspirational Quotes

Perseverance

"It does not matter how slowly you go as long as you do not stop."
– Confucius

Success

"Success usually comes to those who are too busy to be looking for it."
– Henry David Thoreau

Imagination

"Logic will get you from A to B. Imagination will take you everywhere."
– Albert Einstein

Quotes inspire people because they capture wisdom, motivation, and perspective in just a few words, reminding us of the power of thought, perseverance, and creativity.
